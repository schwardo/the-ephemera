The Ephemera is a hotel of 64 rooms arranged as a 4×4×4 cube. Each room's 3-digit number encodes its position (floor, row, column). You may only enter a room if your two keycards can unlock it. Each card has two digits along its top edge and two along its bottom. Overlap the cards so three digits show along the top. Manipulate the keycards to permanently unlock rooms.

Click a green cell adjacent to your current location (blue outline) to walk there. Reach the ★ destination to win.
